# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard is an industry-level Business Intelligence project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and business growth trends using data analytics and visualization techniques.

The project transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through KPI monitoring, trend analysis, forecasting, and interactive dashboards.

## Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
* Evaluate regional and category-wise business performance.
* Track key business KPIs.
* Forecast future sales trends.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

## Features

* Interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard
* Revenue and Profit Tracking
* KPI Monitoring
* Category Performance Analysis
* Regional Sales Analysis
* Top & Bottom Product Analysis
* Customer Insights
* Sales Forecasting
* Business Recommendations
* Executive Summary Reporting
